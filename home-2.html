Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our immigration pathways and client portal dashboards.

Do I need a job offer to apply for Express Entry?

No. Many Express Entry streams (like FSWP) do not require a job offer to submit a profile or receive an ITA. However, a verified offer adds valuable CRS points. Provincial Nominee Programs can also issue nominations without local job offers.

How long does the visa evaluation take?

A standard profile assessment takes approximately 45 to 60 minutes. Your initial roadmap consultation, where we build your points-boosting strategy, takes about 75 minutes.

How many weeks does WES credential evaluation take?

Once WES receives all required university transcripts and documents directly from your home institution, the evaluation typically takes 4 to 6 weeks. We help guide this transcript sourcing process.

What documents should I prepare first?

You should prioritize scheduling your language exam (IELTS/CELPIP/TEF) and initiating your academic credentials assessment (ECA). These take the longest to process and form the baseline of your profile score.
