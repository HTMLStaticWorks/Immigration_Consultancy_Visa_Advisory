Our History

Im Consult Visa Advisory

Founded in 2014 by legal experts and immigration consultants, Im Consult was built to serve global professionals who need clear, transparent visa support. We believed that standard immigration agencies lacked the dynamic, step-by-step digital progress tracking required to make applicants feel secure and confident.

Today, our agency operates a state-of-the-art credential evaluation matching engine, global relocation planning systems, and on-site consultation suites, hosting over 25 immigration attorneys and specialists.
