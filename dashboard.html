Visa Category Resource Guide

Comprehensive requirements, key eligibility metrics, and processing pathways for key global immigration streams.

Express Entry (Federal Skilled Worker)

Canada Skilled Migration
Stream Overview

Canada's primary immigration pathway for skilled workers. Candidates are ranked using the Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) based on age, education, and language.

Key Diagnostic Indicator

Core Benchmarks: CLB 9 Language Proficiency & ECA credential assessment validation.

Standard Processing Timeline

6 to 8 months. Category-based selections draw regularly for STEM, healthcare, and trade candidates.

Student Visa & Study Permit

Academic Residency
Stream Overview

Temporary resident visa allowing international students to study at Designated Learning Institutions (DLI) in Canada, with pathways to post-graduation work permits.

Key Diagnostic Indicator

Core Benchmarks: Letter of Acceptance (LOA) & Guaranteed Investment Certificate (GIC) proof of funds.

Standard Processing Timeline

4 to 8 weeks. Requires comprehensive study plan statement of purpose explaining career progression.

Family Class Sponsorship

Family Unification
Stream Overview

Pathway for citizens and permanent residents to sponsor spouses, common-law partners, or dependent children for permanent residency.

Key Diagnostic Indicator

Core Benchmarks: Verification of relationship authenticity (marriage certificate, cohabitation records, photos).

Standard Processing Timeline

10 to 12 months. Requires sponsor agreement and financial undertaking to support sponsored members.

Visitor Visa (Temporary Resident)

Short-Term Entry
Stream Overview

Entry authorization for tourism, family visits, or short business consultations. Granted for up to 6 months per entry.

Key Diagnostic Indicator

Core Benchmarks: Dual intent demonstration (showing strong ties to home country to ensure return).

Standard Processing Timeline

2 to 4 weeks. Requires invitation letters and travel itineraries matching applicant resources.

Work Permit (LMIA-Based)

Employer-Sponsored
Stream Overview

Authorizes foreign nationals to work for a specific Canadian employer. The employer must first obtain a positive Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA) showing local scarcity.

Key Diagnostic Indicator

Core Benchmarks: Positive LMIA decision number & official job offer detailing wages conforming to regional averages.

Standard Processing Timeline

2 to 3 months. Fast-tracked under the Global Skills Strategy for select tech occupations.
