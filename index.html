Our Immigration Advisory Timeline

A structured, transparent step-by-step roadmap designed to guide you safely from initial evaluation to successful landing.

Phase 1

Profile & Eligibility Assessment

We begin with a detailed eligibility evaluation, calculating your points (CRS/points grid) and identifying the best migration pathways.

Phase 2

Customized Strategy Mapping

We map out your application strategy, detailing required documents, credential evaluations (WES/ECA), and language test goals.

Phase 3

Document Collection & Review

You enter the document compilation phase. We review employment references, proof of funds letters, and verify police clearances.

Phase 4

Profile Submission & Invitation

We submit your profile into pools. Upon receiving the Invitation to Apply (ITA), we review the complete visa file before submission.

Phase 5

Visa Approval & Relocation Support

Final visa stamping and pre-departure briefings. We prepare you for landing checks and provide initial settlement support.
